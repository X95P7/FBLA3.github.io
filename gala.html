The Help One Gala

Step into the enchantment of Help One's Gala at Ivanhoe on March 13th, 2024! Join us for a night of elegance and philanthropy, set against the backdrop of Ivanhoe's timeless charm. This exclusive event promises an evening filled with inspiration, featuring captivating speakers, heartwarming success stories, and live entertainment that will leave you mesmerized. As we gather at this prestigious venue, your presence contributes directly to Help One's mission of creating positive change in our community. The Gala serves as a vital fundraiser, empowering us to continue our impactful projects and initiatives. Elegant attire, delectable cuisine, and an ambiance of goodwill await you at Ivanhoe, creating the perfect setting for a memorable night dedicated to making a meaningful difference. Mark your calendar for March 13th, 2024, and be a part of an unforgettable evening where generosity meets glamor. Your attendance at the Help One Gala not only supports our mission but also adds to the collective spirit of compassion and community service. Join us in transforming lives and building a brighter future together.
