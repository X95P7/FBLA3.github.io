About

Help One is a vibrant high school club based at Mundelein High School, established two years ago with a mission to make a positive impact on the local community. With around 60 dedicated members, Help One is a medium-sized group that channels its energy into various community projects, including donation drives, making blankets, creating heartfelt cards, and organizing gift drives. The club's name, "Help One," reflects its core philosophy of caring for each individual, emphasizing a personal and compassionate approach to community service. Join us in our journey to make a difference, one person at a time.

Mission

At Help One, our mission is simple yet profound: to extend a helping hand and make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need. We are driven by the belief that small acts of kindness can create a ripple effect of positive change. With a focus on community service, we engage in diverse projects that aid our communities. Our ultimate goal is to embody the spirit of compassion and empathy, striving to make a difference in the lives of individuals within our local community - making the world a better place, one act of kindness at a time.
